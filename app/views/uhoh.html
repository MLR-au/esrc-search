Unfortunately, in the process of developing this application we've had to make some decisions about which types of devices and browser versions we could reasonably support.

If you're reading this then it means that either your device is too small for this application (it wouldn't work very well) or your browser is too old. If the issue is your browser, then may we suggest you take a look at Browse Happy to find out which browsers are available and perhaps, get a newer version of the one you're using.

Alternately, you might like to try another browser in which case we can recommend both Google Chrome and Mozilla Firefox as suitable for this application.
