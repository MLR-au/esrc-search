A basic search box.

  • Multiple words are treated as a phrase.
  • Fuzzy searching is NOT enabled; unless:
    • If there are no documents, a spell check is triggered and a suggestion is provided.
    • If it's a single word - then a fuzzy search is also performed on the word otherwise the current result set is blanked and only the suggestion is shown (fuzzy search only works on single works - not phrases)
      • The spell checker ain't great... See 'balarat' (good) and 'orpanage' (bad).
  • Search is performed against the 'name' and 'text' fields.
    • Matches in the 'name' field carry 10 times the weight of matches in the text field.
  • Each result comes with 3 'More Like This' where similarity of terms is used to suggest similar documents.
